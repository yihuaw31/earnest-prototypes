The problem

You're not confused about money.
You're confused about which life to choose.

You've done the hard part — you're saving, earning well, maybe even ahead of schedule. But the next set of questions don't have obvious answers.

🌍

"Can we actually move to another country and keep our home here?"

The spreadsheet gets complicated fast — rental income, foreign taxes, cost of living gaps. You want to know if it even makes sense before you fall in love with the idea.
👶

"What does having a baby do to our finances over the next 10 years?"

Not just the stroller. Childcare, lost income, school decisions, home size, career pauses. You want the honest number, not the optimistic one.
🏖️

"How much more do we need to actually retire early?"

You've heard about FIRE but your situation is messier — equity, mortgage, maybe kids. You want a real answer, not a rule of thumb from Reddit.

These aren't questions about discipline. They're questions about illegibility — you can't see how the decision unfolds over time, so you stay frozen. Paths makes it visible.

How it works

Three steps to seeing clearly.

🔗
1

Connect your financial picture

Link income, savings, investments, real estate, and any debt. Paths builds a complete model of where you stand today — not a guess, your actual numbers.

🤔
2

Describe the decisions you're weighing

Tell Paths what's on your mind: "We're thinking about moving to Lisbon for two years" or "We want to have our first kid in the next 18 months." No financial jargon needed.

🛤️
3

See your paths side by side

Paths models each scenario across 10 years — with plain-language summaries, not just charts. You see the tradeoffs clearly enough to actually commit to a direction.
