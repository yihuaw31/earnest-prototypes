How it works

Tend your finances the way
you'd tend a garden.

01

Connect your accounts.

Grove reads your financial picture — savings, debt, spending, income. No manual entry. No spreadsheets. Just a quiet, honest look at where things stand.

02

Meet your garden.

Every part of your finances becomes part of the landscape. You see your financial health the way you'd look out a window — at a glance, without anxiety. No scores. No red numbers.

03

Watch it grow.

Every extra dollar saved, every debt payment made, every steady week — the garden responds. Slowly, honestly, beautifully. The garden is always yours. It just grows at different speeds.

The garden, explained

Every corner has meaning.

🌳
The tree
Your long-term savings. Grows slowly and steadily. The older it gets, the harder it is to uproot.
🌱
The ground cover
Your emergency fund. How protected the soil is. Thicker coverage means you can weather anything.
🌸
The flowers
Your spending health. Blooming when you're within your means, quietly fading when things get tight.
🌿
The weeds
High-interest debt. Still there, visible, honest — but visibly smaller every time you pay something down.
